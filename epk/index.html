Teeth Kids

Information & Resources

Teeth Kids is a 6-piece sludge metal outfit from Chicago, IL formed in 2022.

Their first release, "Is This Loss b/w Weight", was released in 2024.

Their first LP is complete, will be announced shortly, and will be released in spring/summer of 2026.

Both were recorded by the much loved & missed Simon Small at Tunnel of Reverb here in Chicago.

Teeth Kids crafts songs of anguish and dread, channeling traumas of the past and concerns of the present. Their songs & soundscapes are challenging but connective, foreboding but hopeful, punishing yet parseable.

Members

  • Cam Daigle: Vocals
  • Clementine Baker: Bass
  • Cooper Glodoski: Drums
  • Emily Rowen: Guitar
  • June Stutz: Synth
  • Sawyer Hildebrandt: Guitar
